סכמת FAQ חדשה (5 שאלות)

ש: Do prices on the website include all costs?
ת: Yes, website prices are "on the road" prices including VAT, registration fee, and road preparation. Not included: comprehensive insurance, annual mandatory insurance (~600 NIS), ownership registration (~300 NIS).
ש: What financing options are available?
ת: Bank financing from Prime + 0.5%, private leasing with fixed monthly payment, or direct payments through the importer. Down payment: 10-30% depending on the track.
ש: Are there promotions or discounts?
ת: Yes - trade-in benefits, end of year/quarter promotions, and special terms for leasing customers.
ש: How do Chery prices compare to competitors?
ת: Tiggo 8 Pro (7 seats) is 30-40K NIS cheaper than Skoda Kodiaq. FX is ~15K NIS cheaper than Hyundai Kona with similar equipment.
ש: What's included vs optional?
ת: Standard: large screens, 360 cameras, panoramic roof, power driver seat, adaptive cruise control, advanced safety. Optional: special colors (~2,500 NIS), real leather, premium audio.
