Q: What industries does Admati Agencies serve?

A: Admati Agencies specializes in components and sub-systems for the semiconductor, medical device, industrial automation, and defense industries, providing turnkey solutions from design to delivery.

Q: Where is Admati Agencies located?

A: Admati Agencies is headquartered in Haifa, Israel (P.O. Box 7035, 3107001), with international partnerships enabling worldwide service and door-to-door delivery.

Q: What makes Admati different from other component suppliers?

Q: What semiconductor testing services does Admati offer?

A: Admati provides comprehensive IC qualification testing including ESD (Electrostatic Discharge), HTOL (High Temperature Operating Life), HAST (Highly Accelerated Stress Test), and other reliability tests per industry standards.

Q: What is IC Fast Assembly?

A: IC Fast Assembly is Admati's rapid microelectronic packaging service including wafer preparation, die attach, wire bonding, and final packaging with quick turnaround times for prototypes and small production runs.

Q: Does Admati supply ATE (Automatic Test Equipment)?

A: Yes, Admati provides complete ATE solutions including test handlers, probe cards, burn-in sockets, loadboards, and thermal management equipment from leading manufacturers.

Q: What thermal testing solutions are available?

A: Admati offers temperature forcing systems, thermal chambers, and thermal management solutions for semiconductor testing from -65°C to +300°C ranges.

Q: Can Admati support device qualification for automotive applications?

A: Yes, Admati's qualification labs support automotive-grade testing per AEC-Q100/Q101 standards, including extended temperature range and reliability testing for automotive semiconductors.

Q: What are pogo pins used for?

A: Pogo pins (spring-loaded contact pins) provide reliable temporary electrical connections for semiconductor testing, battery charging, medical devices, wearables, and industrial automation applications.

Q: What pogo pin configurations does Admati offer?

A: Admati offers thru-hole, surface-mount, solder-cup, and cable-mount configurations in various sizes, with customizable spring forces, travel heights, and current ratings.

Q: How many mating cycles can pogo pins withstand?

A: Admati's high-quality pogo pins are rated for hundreds of thousands of mating cycles, featuring hard gold over nickel plating for maximum durability and conductivity.

Q: Can Admati provide custom pogo pin solutions?

A: Yes, Admati engineers custom pogo pin solutions with specific length, diameter, spring force, and material requirements to meet exact application needs.

Q: Are waterproof pogo pin connectors available?

A: Yes, Admati supplies IP-rated waterproof pogo pin connectors designed for medical devices, outdoor equipment, and harsh industrial environments.

Q: What are Admati's business hours?

A: Admati operates Sunday through Friday, 10:00 AM to 5:00 PM Israel time (IST). The office is closed on Saturdays and Israeli holidays.

Q: How can I contact the Semiconductor division?

A: Contact Alex Belkin directly at +972 50 535 3644 for semiconductor-related inquiries including IC testing, ATE equipment, and device qualification.

Q: Who handles Pogo Pin inquiries?

A: Yonatan Adamti manages the Pogo Pins division and can be reached at +972 50 526 3333 for spring-loaded connector and custom pin solutions.

Q: How do I request a quote for mechanical parts?

A: Contact Sophia Abramson in the Mechanical Division at +972 50 678 4700, or submit details through the contact form selecting "Mechanical" as the department.

Q: How quickly does Admati respond to inquiries?

A: Admati typically responds to all inquiries within 24-48 business hours. Urgent requests can be addressed by calling the main line at +972 50 331 4700.
