Required Actions

1

Create FAQ Section on Homepage

Action: Add a visible FAQ section with 5-7 common questions. THEN add FAQPage schema.

Why: You cannot add FAQ schema without visible FAQ content (Google guidelines violation).

Suggested FAQs:

  • What is MontoPay?
  • How does MontoPay integrate with AP portals?
  • What portals does MontoPay connect to?
  • How much can I save with AR automation?
  • How long does implementation take?
2

Add Blog Article Links to Homepage Footer

Action: Add a "From Our Blog" or "Resources" section linking to top articles.

Why: Currently no blog links on homepage. This improves internal linking and keeps users on site.

Recommended articles to feature:

  • Vendor Billing Automation Guide (position #1 page)
  • Accounts Receivable Automation Guide (pillar)
  • Complete Directory of Supplier Payment Portals
3

Get G2/Capterra Reviews (Prerequisite for Review Schema)

Action: Create G2 profile, request reviews from verified customers (AppsFlyer, Shutterstock, TechTarget, Miro, G2, Yotpo).

Why: You cannot add Review/AggregateRating schema without third-party verified reviews. Google prohibits self-serving review schema.

Timeline: 30-60 days to accumulate reviews, then implement schema.

4

Remove BreadcrumbList from Homepage

Action: BreadcrumbList schema should only be on internal pages, not homepage.

Why: Homepage IS the first item in breadcrumbs. Having breadcrumb schema on homepage is redundant and can confuse crawlers.
